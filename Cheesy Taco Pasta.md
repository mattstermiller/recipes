# Cheesy Taco Pasta

## Ingredients

- 12 oz. short-cut pasta
- 1/2 large white onion, chopped
- Salt to taste
- 1/2 jalapeño, chopped
- 1 lb. ground beef
- 2 tbsp. taco seasoning
- 1 (15-oz.) can diced fire-roasted tomatoes
- 1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar and monterey jack
- Chopped fresh cilantro, for garnish

## Directions

1. In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook pasta according to package directions until al dente. Drain and return to pot.
2. Meanwhile, in a large skillet over medium heat, heat oil. Add onion and season with salt. Cook until tender, 5 minutes. Add jalapeño, cook 2 minutes more.
3. Add ground beef and taco seasoning, cook until beef is no longer pink. Drain fat.
4. Add canned tomatoes and 1/4 cup water and stir, then add cheese and let melt, stirring occasionally, 2 minutes. Add cooked pasta and toss until completely combined.
5. Garnish with cilantro and serve.
