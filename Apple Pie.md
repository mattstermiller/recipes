# Apple Pie

## Ingredients

- 1 box Pillsbury refrigerated pie crusts, softened as directed on box
- 6 cups thinly sliced, peeled apples (6 medium-sized)
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
- 3/4 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp ground nutmeg
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

## Directions

1. Heat oven to 400°F. Place 1 pie crust in ungreased 9" glass pie plate. Press firmly against side and bottom.
2. In large bowl, gently mix filling ingredients; spoon into crust-lined pie plate. Top with second crust. Wrap excess
   top crust under bottom crust edge, pressing edges together to seal; flute. Cut slits or shapes in several places in
   top crust.
3. Bake 40 to 45 minutes or until apples are tender and crust is golden brown. Cover edge of crust with 2" to 3" wide
   strips of foil after first 15 to 20 minutes of baking to prevent excessive browning. Cool on cooling rack at least 2
   hours before serving.
