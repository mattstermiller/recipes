# Taco Soup

## Ingredients

- 1.5 lb ground beef
- 1 carton beef broth
- 2 cans black beans
- 1 can fire-roasted tomatoes
- 3 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 packet taco seasoning
- 1 packet ranch seasoning
- 1 tbsp minced onion
- 1 tbsp minced garlic
- shredded cheese
- tortilla strips (or fritos)
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Brown ground beef with minced onion and garlic; Drain
2. Mix ground beef, beef broth, beans, tomatoes, tomato paste, and seasoning packets in slow cooker
3. Cook on low for 4 hours or high for 2 hours
4. Serve into bowls and add shredded cheese, tortilla strips and sour cream
